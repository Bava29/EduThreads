Measurement Guide

How To Measure Correctly

Follow these simple measurement steps to ensure your child gets the perfect uniform fit for everyday comfort.

01

Chest

Measure around the fullest part of the chest while keeping the tape comfortably snug.

02

Waist

Measure around the natural waistline without pulling the tape too tight.

03

Height

Measure from the floor to the top of the head while standing straight.

04

Sleeve Length

Measure from the shoulder to the wrist with the arm slightly bent.

Helpful Tips

Measure With Confidence

Follow these simple tips to ensure the perfect fit before placing your order.

Use A Clear & Soft Tape

Always use a flexible and clean measuring tape for accurate body measurements.

Stand Naturally Straight

Measure while standing naturally straight with arms relaxed for the best results.

Choose One Size Up

If your measurements fall between two sizes, choose the one that larger size.

Compare Uniform

Compare measurements with an already existing well-fitting school uniform.
